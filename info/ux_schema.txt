UX Schema Guidelines

1. Visual Hierarchy
- Clean, professional design
- Education-focused imagery
- Saudi cultural elements
- Clear CTAs
- Mobile-first approach

2. Color Scheme
Primary:
- Blue (#2B6CB0) - Trust & Education
- Orange (#ED8936) - Action & Energy
Secondary:
- White (#FFFFFF) - Clean space
- Gray (#4A5568) - Text
- Green (#48BB78) - Success/WhatsApp

3. Typography
- Arabic: Cairo font family
- English: Inter font family
- Hierarchy:
  * H1: 2.5rem
  * H2: 2rem
  * Body: 1rem
  * CTAs: 1.125rem

4. Components
Opt-in Page:
- Sticky header
- Hero section
- Benefits grid
- Form component
- Social proof
- Footer

Sales Page:
- Video player
- Feature cards
- Pricing table
- FAQ accordion
- WhatsApp button
- Payment section

Thank You Page:
- Success message
- Next steps cards
- Resource links
- Support contact

5. Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: 320px
- Tablet: 768px
- Desktop: 1024px
- Large: 1280px

6. Loading States
- Skeleton screens
- Progress indicators
- Form submission states
- Payment processing states

7. Accessibility
- WCAG 2.1 compliance
- RTL support
- High contrast options
- Screen reader optimization
